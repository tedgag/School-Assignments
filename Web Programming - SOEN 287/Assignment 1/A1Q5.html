Best lanes to play How to play them
  1. Jungle
  1. Best champions to play
    • Sejuani
    • Warwick
    • Shyvana
  2. Tips and tricks
    • Try to be in the other jungler's head at all times
    • When you gank someone and they use flash, try to immediatly gank again
    • Objectives are better than kills, focus on securing dragons with your team
  1. Mid
  1. Best champions to play
    • Ahri
    • Zed
    • Katarina
  2. Tips and tricks
    • At level 6, either shove your lane and roam bot/top or all-in your oponnent
    • Ward one side of the river and stick to it to avoid ganks
    • Punish your oponnent when he goes to cs or when he misses some spells
